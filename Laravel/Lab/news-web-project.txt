	- name
+ table news
	- id
	- category_id (FK)
	- authors_id (FK)
	- title
	- description
	- status ENUM('public', 'unpublic')
	- thumbnail
+ table article 
	- id (PK)
	- news_id (FK)
	- content
+table ImageOfArticle
	- id (PK)
	-article_id (FK)

Relationship
+ Category with News ( one-to-many )
	- one category has many news
	- news belongs to one category
+ Author and News ( one-to-many or many-to-many ) if use many to many should be have linking table.
	- one author writes many news
	- news belongs to one author
+ News and Article ( one-to-many )
	- one news has one or many articles
	- articles belongs to one news
+ Article and ImageOfArticle
	- One article has many images
	- Image belongs to one article

Commonly Used Faker Methods in Laravel
Below is a selection of commonly used methods in Laravel factories and seeders: 
Person & Contact
$faker->name – Generates a full name.
$faker->firstName – Generates a first name.
$faker->lastName – Generates a last name.
$faker->title – Generates a title (e.g., 'Mr.', 'Mrs.').
$faker->email – Generates an email address.
$faker->unique()->safeEmail – Generates a unique, safe email address.
$faker->phoneNumber – Generates a phone number. 
Address & Location
$faker->address – Generates a full address.
$faker->streetAddress – Generates a street address.
$faker->city – Generates a city name.
$faker->postcode – Generates a postal code.
$faker->country – Generates a country name.
$faker->latitude, $faker->longitude – Generates geographical coordinates. 
Text & Lorem Ipsum
$faker->word – Generates a single word.
$faker->words(3, true) – Generates an array of words, or a string if the second argument is true.
$faker->sentence – Generates a random sentence.
$faker->paragraph – Generates a random paragraph.
$faker->text – Generates a larger block of text. 
Numbers & Dates
$faker->randomDigit – Generates a single random digit (0-9).
$faker->randomNumber(5) – Generates a random number with a specific number of digits.
$faker->numberBetween(1, 100) – Generates a number within a specified range.
$faker->randomFloat(2, 0, 100) – Generates a random float with specified decimal places and range.
$faker->date() – Generates a date string.
$faker->dateTime() – Generates a DateTime object.
$faker->year – Generates a year. 
Internet & Miscellaneous
$faker->userName – Generates a username.
$faker->password – Generates a random password.
$faker->domainName – Generates a domain name.
$faker->url – Generates a URL.
$faker->imageUrl() – Generates a URL for a placeholder image.
$faker->uuid – Generates a UUID.
$faker->boolean – Generates true or false.
$faker->randomElement(['seller', 'buyer']) – Selects a random value from a provided array.
